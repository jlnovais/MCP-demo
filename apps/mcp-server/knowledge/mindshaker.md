# Mindshaker — Digital Creativity for Culture and Business

## About Us

Founded in 2007 by João Paulo Almeida and Luís Correia, Mindshaker is a digital agency with extensive experience in web/mobile programming, digital strategy, creativity, web design, and consulting. The team consists of 13 professionals and develops national and international projects in various sectors.

Since 2022, it has been an agency integrated into the Plus Company ecosystem, a group comprised of over 26 agencies and 3,000 employees, with 31 offices in 17 countries across Europe, the Middle East, Asia-Pacific, and North America.

We work for and with clients, whether agencies or brands, combining skills and significantly accelerating the pace of implementation. Mindshaker's philosophy allows us to view work as a collaborative process that can and should be continuously improved.

We are technologically agnostic. We choose technology based on the problem to be solved and use cutting-edge technology to deliver reliable, secure, and scalable solutions.

Every day we put into practice the values of Trust, Transparency, and Independence, promoting a culture of continuous learning, collaboration, and excellence, empowering our clients to thrive in the digital age.

Our journey over nearly two decades has allowed us to refine a flexible workflow that easily adapts to the evolving needs of both local businesses and global enterprises. By bridging the gap between artistic creativity and robust engineering, we ensure that every digital product we deploy not only looks exceptional but functions flawlessly under heavy demands. We don't just build websites and applications; we engineer digital experiences that foster meaningful connections.

As part of the Plus Company ecosystem, we leverage a massive global network of insights and specialized talent while retaining our core identity as a boutique, agile agency. This unique position allows us to scale our operations rapidly for large-scale international campaigns, giving our clients the strategic muscle of a multinational corporation paired with the dedicated, hands-on attention of a local partner.

Looking toward the future, we remain committed to exploring the outer fringes of digital innovation, from immersive web experiences to AI-driven integrations. Our team is constantly prototyping new concepts and testing emerging frameworks, ensuring that when our clients are ready to take their next digital leap, we already have the blueprint, the tools, and the expertise ready to guide them.

Our collaborative footprint spans across culturally diverse markets, allowing us to deeply understand how different audiences interact with digital touchpoints. This global perspective influences our localization strategies, ensuring that the software and platforms we build are accessible, inclusive, and highly relevant to users regardless of their geographic location.

Through a highly selective hiring process, we have brought together a multidisciplinary hub of developers, designers, and strategists who are masters of their respective crafts. This concentrated expertise enables us to bypass traditional agency bureaucracy, creating a direct line of communication between our creators and our clients for rapid prototyping and zero friction.

Ultimately, our longevity in a hyper-competitive market stands as a testament to our adaptability and dedication. We continuously reinvest in our internal infrastructure and knowledge base, positioning Mindshaker not just as a service provider, but as a long-term strategic ally capable of navigating the complex digital landscape alongside your business.

## Philosophy

Mindshaker is the attitude that characterizes us; it's how we approach the challenges presented to us. We question everything and everyone, deconstructing ideas to go further and guarantee the perfect solution for your business. Only in this way can we meet market demands. Added to this attitude is the unique relationship we build with our clients, the result of investing in long-term relationships.

Mindshaker is a process of sharing and aligning ideas. Everything is put out there, without fear of judgment; we listen to the ideas of others and build upon them based on mutual inspiration and feedback. In this way, we grow and help others grow.

For all these reasons, we are passionate about what we do, and it is this passion that drives and attracts us.

Being a Mindshaker means working with positive energy, not calling what we do work, and achieving a balance between personal and professional life.

All this with surfing running through our veins. We are located on the Costa da Caparica, where we can bring together what we need to be successful as a company, professionals, and individuals.

We believe that true creativity cannot thrive in a rigid, corporate vacuum. Just like reading the ocean before catching a wave, navigating the digital landscape requires intuition, patience, and the ability to pivot at a moment's notice. We embrace the unpredictable, turning unexpected technical or creative hurdles into opportunities for breakthrough innovation.

For us, transparency isn’t just a corporate buzzword; it is the cornerstone of our daily interactions. We believe in honest conversations, setting realistic expectations, and celebrating victories together with our partners. By demystifying complex technologies and keeping our processes open, we build an environment of absolute trust where bold, experimental ideas can safely come to life.

Ultimately, we measure our success not just by the lines of code we write or the awards we win, but by the lasting impact we leave on the communities and businesses we serve. We ride the line between discipline and freedom, knowing that the best ideas are born when you are grounded in your expertise but free enough to catch the next big swell.

This fluid mindset guides how we look at design and programming—not as rigid rules, but as dynamic mediums meant to be shaped, refined, and pushed to their limits. We encourage our team to break conventional patterns because true distinction in the digital sphere belongs to those who dare to stand out and offer something genuinely refreshing.

Our relationship with the sea teaches us humility and respect for natural forces, a philosophy we directly translate into our approach to market trends and technological shifts. Rather than blindly chasing every passing fad, we carefully study the currents, ensuring that the strategic directions we recommend have substance, longevity, and real value.

In the end, Mindshaker is a collective pulse—a shared belief that work should elevate the human experience. By nurturing an environment where individuality is celebrated and collective ambition is standard, we don't just deliver successful projects; we cultivate a legacy of digital craftsmanship that inspires everyone involved.
